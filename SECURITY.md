# Security Policy

This project is defensive and intended for authorized risk-control, anti-abuse, anti-fraud, trust & safety, and business-security work.

## Do Not Use This Skill For

- fraud operations
- bypassing risk controls
- bulk account registration
- credential attacks
- scraping or automation abuse
- fake transactions, fake reviews, or cash-out operations
- locating live black-market resources

## Responsible Use

Use only on systems, datasets, and investigations where you have authorization. Do not include personal data, credentials, secrets, or sensitive internal case details in public issues.

## Reporting Issues

For safety or misuse-enabling behavior, open a private security advisory if the hosting platform supports it, or contact the maintainers privately. Include:

- prompt/input that caused the issue
- observed output
- why it is unsafe or incorrect
- expected defensive behavior
